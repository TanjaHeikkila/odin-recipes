Spicy Fried Chickpeas

Description

Golden, crispy chickpeas tossed in bold spices — perfect as a snack, salad cruncher, or secret dinner weapon. Legend says one handful and you're hooked for life.

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 1 ts smoked paprika powder
  • 1 tb dried and grounded garlig
  • 1/2 ts salt
  • 1 pinch cumin

Steps

  1. Dry and ready: Rinse canned chickpeas, then pat them bone-dry with a towel — moisture is the enemy of crispiness.
  2. Sizzle time: Fry chickpeas in hot olive oil until golden and popping (about 10–15 minutes).
  3. Spice storm: Toss with smoked paprika, cayenne, cumin, garlic powder, and sea salt.
  4. Cool & crunch: Let them rest on paper towels, then devour like spicy popcorn.
