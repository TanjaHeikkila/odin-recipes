Stuffed Peppers with Beef

Description

Bell peppers filled with savory ground beef, rice, and melty cheese — a comfort classic that tastes like a hug in a roasted shell. It’s the kind of meal that makes neighbors jealous.

Ingredients

  • 6 bell peppers, any color
  • 3 cups chunky tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, very thinly sliced
  • ½ cup beef broth
  • 1/2 ts red pepper flakes
  • 1 pounds ground beef
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup freshly shredded cheese
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh flat-leaf parsley chopped
  • 1 ts salt and black pepper or to taste

Steps

  1. Prep the peppers: Cut off tops, scoop out seeds, and lightly roast or blanch the peppers to soften.
  2. Brown the beef: Sauté ground beef with onion and garlic until golden.
  3. Flavor bomb: Stir in tomato paste, cooked rice, herbs (oregano, thyme), and salt & pepper.
  4. Cheese magic: Mix in shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or whatever you love).
  5. Fill 'em up: Stuff the peppers generously with the beef-rice mixture.
  6. Top it off: Sprinkle with more cheese or breadcrumbs for a golden crown.
  7. Bake to glory: Place in a baking dish, cover with foil, and bake at 190 °C (375 °F) for 25–30 min.
  8. Final melt: Remove foil for the last 5–10 min to brown the top. Serve warm and proud.
